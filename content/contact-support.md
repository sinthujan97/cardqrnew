# Contact Support

Have a question, found a bug, or need help with your card? We're here to help.

## Get in Touch

**Email:** support@getcardqr.com

We aim to respond within 1–2 business days.

## Common Questions

Before reaching out, you might find a quick answer in our [FAQ section](/#faq), covering:
- How to edit your card after printing your QR code
- Whether cards expire
- Custom branding options
- How QR scanning works without an app

## Report an Issue

If you've found a bug, broken template, or an issue with a generated QR code, please include:
- The template type you were using (Business Card, Menu, Event, Link Hub, WiFi, or Catalog)
- A description of what happened
- Your browser and device type, if relevant

## Business & Partnership Inquiries

For partnership, press, or business inquiries, please use the same email above with "Business Inquiry" in the subject line.
