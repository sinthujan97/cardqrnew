# Free Digital Business Card Maker

Paper business cards get lost, run out at the worst moment, and can't be updated once printed. A digital business card fixes all three — share it with a QR code or a link, and update your details anytime without reprinting.

## How a digital business card works

You create a page with your name, job title, company, photo, and contact links (phone, email, LinkedIn, website, etc.). CardQR generates a QR code linking to that page. Anyone who scans it sees your card instantly in their browser — no app required on their end.

Many digital business cards also support **Save Contact**, letting the other person add you straight to their phone's contacts with one tap, instead of manually typing your number.

## Why switch from paper cards

- **Never run out** — a QR code on a phone screen or printed card back works every time
- **Always up to date** — changed jobs or numbers? Edit the card, the same QR code now points to your latest details
- **More than a phone number** — link your portfolio, LinkedIn, Calendly, or website, none of which fits on a paper card
- **Track engagement** — see when your card is being scanned (availability depends on your plan)

## Where to use your digital business card

- Printed on the back of a minimalist physical card
- As your email signature link
- On a conference badge or lanyard
- In your LinkedIn or Instagram bio
