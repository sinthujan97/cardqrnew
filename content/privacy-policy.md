# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 20, 2026**

CardQR ("CardQR," "we," "us," or "our") operates getcardqr.com (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Service.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
When you create a card, menu, event page, WiFi card, link hub, or catalog using CardQR, you may enter information such as names, job titles, contact details, photos, prices, event details, WiFi network names and passwords, or links. This information is used solely to generate your card and is stored to allow you to edit your card later using your private edit link.

### Automatically Collected Information
When you visit our Service, we may automatically collect:
- Device and browser type
- IP address (used for general location and security purposes)
- Pages visited and time spent on the Service
- Referring website

### Cookies and Similar Technologies
We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate and improve our Service. This includes:
- Essential cookies required for the Service to function
- Analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our Service
- Advertising cookies, including those used by Google AdSense, to serve relevant ads

### Google AdSense and Advertising
We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our Service. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our Service and/or other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google's Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/). You can also opt out of third-party vendor use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting [www.aboutads.info](http://www.aboutads.info/).

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps, visit [Google's Privacy & Terms page](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:
- Generate and host the cards, menus, and pages you create
- Allow you to edit your content using your private edit link
- Operate, maintain, and improve our Service
- Display advertisements through Google AdSense
- Monitor usage and detect, prevent, or address technical issues or abuse
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:
- **Service providers** who help us operate the Service (e.g. hosting providers)
- **Advertising partners** (Google AdSense) as described above
- **Legal authorities** if required by law, or to protect our rights, users, or the public

## Data Retention

Cards and content you create remain stored on our servers until you delete them or the content is removed in accordance with our Terms of Service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

## Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## International Data Transfers

CardQR is operated from Sri Lanka and may use hosting and service providers located in other countries, including the United States. By using our Service, you consent to the transfer of your information to these locations.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
