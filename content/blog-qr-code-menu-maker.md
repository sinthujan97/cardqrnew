# Free QR Code Menu Maker for Restaurants

Printed menus get outdated the moment a price changes or a dish sells out. A QR code menu solves this — your menu lives online, and updating it instantly updates what every table sees, without reprinting a single page.

## Why restaurants switch to QR menus

- **Update prices and items instantly** — no reprinting when a seasonal item changes or a price moves
- **Lower printing costs** — one small QR code per table replaces full printed menu runs
- **Faster table turnover** — guests browse and decide while waiting, without needing a server to bring a physical menu
- **Multi-language friendly** — easier to maintain translated versions when the menu lives in one editable place

## What a good QR code menu needs

A QR menu should feel as easy to read as a printed one. At minimum, look for:
- Clear categories (starters, mains, desserts, drinks)
- Visible pricing next to each item
- Optional photos for signature dishes
- Fast loading on mobile data, not just WiFi — guests scanning from outside a venue may be on cellular data

- A small table tent or sticker at each table
- Printed on the back of a physical reduced/seasonal card
- At the entrance, for guests to browse while waiting

No — CardQR hosts your menu page for you. You don't need your own website or hosting.

Yes. The QR code points to your menu page, which you can edit anytime through your private edit link. The printed code stays the same.
