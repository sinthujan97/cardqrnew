# About CardQR

CardQR exists to make one specific moment faster: the moment between scanning a code and getting the information you actually need.

## What we build

We make digital destinations for QR codes — pages that feel like part of an app, not a slow website. Right now, CardQR supports six core templates:

- **Business Card** — share contact details with one scan, Save Contact built in
- **Restaurant Menu** — categorized items, prices, and photos that update instantly
- **Event Card** — dates, locations, agendas, and RSVP collection in one place
- **Link Hub** — a premium alternative to link-in-bio tools
- **WiFi Sharing** — tap-to-copy network credentials, no typing required
- **Product Catalog** — a simple grid with prices and direct WhatsApp ordering

## Why we built it this way

Most QR tools either generate a static code with nothing useful behind it, or they require a full account, onboarding flow, and subscription before you can create anything. We built CardQR around a simpler idea: pick a template, fill it in, get your QR code — no account required to start.

Every card stays editable through a private edit link, so a printed QR code never needs reprinting just because a price, date, or phone number changed.

## Who uses CardQR

CardQR is used by individuals creating networking cards and link hubs, and by small businesses — restaurants, cafés, event organizers, and local shops — who need a fast, professional digital presence without building a website.

## Our approach to design

Every template is built mobile-first, since the entire flow begins with a phone camera. We prioritize fast load times, clean print-ready QR exports, and a card experience that feels native, not like a clunky redirect.
